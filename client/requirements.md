## Packages
gsap | Premium animations, scroll effects, and micro-interactions
@hookform/resolvers | Form validation with Zod
react-hook-form | Form state management
clsx | Utility for constructing className strings conditionally
tailwind-merge | Utility for merging Tailwind classes

## Notes
- GSAP is used extensively for page transitions, scroll reveals, and hover effects.
- The UI follows a minimal, high-end editorial design with Playfair Display (serif) for headings and DM Sans (sans-serif) for body text.
- Projects are expected to have valid image URLs in the database.
